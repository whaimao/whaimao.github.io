Personal profile

  Dr. Lei Lei is an Assistant Professor and Master tutor in the Department of Architectural Environment and Equipment Engineering at Zhejiang Sci-Tech University. She is a member of Zhejiang Institute of Refrigeration.

Lei Lei graduated from Department of Heating, Ventilation and Air Conditioning, Dalian University of Technology.

 

Main research directions

1. Pollution control and indoor health;

2. Building ventilation and environmental regulation;

3. Air purification (mechanical, electromagnetic, chemical and other means);

4. Aircraft cockpit, cabin environment control;

5. Building heat transfer and energy consumption simulation;

6. Building energy consumption analysis based on data mining technology.
